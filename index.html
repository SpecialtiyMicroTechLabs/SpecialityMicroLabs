About Us

Speciality Microtech (SML) Lab was started on 30 th July 2006 as first exclusive Microbiology Lab in Gujarat with aim to providing world class diagnostic service in area of Microbiology. Since day of inception, SML has established itself as a well-reputed referral center for Microbiology.


Dr.Bhavin Kapadiya is Lab Director of the Speciality Microtech Lab. By Qualification he is M.D in Microbiology. Graduation and Post graduation are done from B.J. Medical College Ahmedabad. After Post graduation he trained at P.D.Hinduja National Hospital, Mumbai.
